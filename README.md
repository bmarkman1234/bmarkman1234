I'm a geospatial professional passionate about using maps, data, and remote sensing to better understand and manage our environment 🌍

My work sits at the intersection of GIS, remote sensing, and environmental science. I have built workflows with UAV LiDAR, hyperspectral imagery, transportation planning, and field data to support real-world management decisions. Especially interested in where geospatial meets emerging tech.

M.S. Geography, San Diego State University · B.S. Conservation Biology, UC Davis · FAA Part 107 Certified Drone Pilot

### Selected Projects

[**Remote Sensing of Residual Dry Matter**](https://github.com/bmarkman1234/Remote-Sensing-of-Residual-Dry-Matter) — MS thesis research estimating biomass across conservation grazing lands using UAV LiDAR, field spectroscopy, and machine learning. Published in *Remote Sensing* (2025).

[**EnviroZoneLocate**](https://github.com/bmarkman1234/envirozonelocate) — ArcGIS Pro custom geoprocessing toolbox for environmental zone screening and map-based planning workflows.

[**WalkSD**](https://bmarkman1234.github.io/walkSD/) — Interactive walkability mapping app for San Diego neighborhood access and planning exploration.

[**IndexViewer**](https://bmarkman1234.github.io/IndexViewer/) — Lightweight tool for visualizing and comparing spectral indices from satellite and UAV imagery.

[**sdTerra**](https://github.com/bmarkman1234/sdTerra) — R-based relief map combining PRISM climate data, USGS elevation, and terrain exposure proxy.
